AudioDots allows site owners to stream their content as audio to their audience on the go, over mobile and connected devices.

== Description ==
 
Let your audience LISTEN to your content!
Generate and broadcast mobile AUDIO.
AudioDots WordPress plugin lets users listen to the articles on the go, delivering high-fidelity human-like voices reading your blog entries or news articles in a continuous feed.
Now your readers can listen to you when they are driving, riding their bike, strolling in the park, or cooking dinner.
With AudioDots plugin installed, your users just need to click a button to hear your newly generated audio content stream out - just like a radio broadcast or podcast - save the production cost and effort.
Features and Benefits:

- Free tier allows for lifetime posting of up to 4 articles, up to 2,000 words per month.
- Subscription programs available for larger sites. contact us for details.
- More than 220 high fidelity, human-like voices across 40+ languages and variants, including gender.
- Responsive player fits to every page on any supporting device 
- Seamless ad supported streaming, and pre-roll ad insertions.
- Revenue share option for high volume publishers 
- Audio analytics.
- Live chat support Sunday - Thursday. Next day email support.
- Player position can be positioned anywhere above or below title 

== FAQ ==
= Is the plugin free? =
Yes, up to a limit of 4 posts and 2,000 words per month.
= Do you insert ads into the stream? =
We may insert ads, based on demand from our partners. The ads we insert are IAB compliant.
= Do I need to accept your advertiser-publisher agreement to use the plugin? =
Yes. Find it here: https://www.audiodots.com/publisherterms
= Can I define the position of the player and buttons? =
Yes, you can move the player strip up or down. The strip occupies the full width of the text. Once the button pressed, the player appears, substituting the text with controls and status bar.
= I run a large site, and post more than the limit set for the free tier. What do you offer? =
We offer paid subscriptions for larger sites:
    • Pro: Up to 20 posts and 10,000 words per month at $9.90, paid annually
    • Bulletin:	Up to 50 posts and 25,000 words per month at $19.90, paid annually
    • Press: Large press publications with higher content volume – contact us
    • Do you offer revenue sharing to high volume?
Yes. Contact us for details.
